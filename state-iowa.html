Common Iowa Questions

Do you accept boats that are not running?

Often, yes. Condition matters, but a stalled engine, old electronics, expired registration, or cosmetic wear does not automatically rule out a donation.

What types of boats can I submit?

Powerboats, sailboats, cruisers, fishing boats, yachts, personal watercraft, trailers, and documented vessels can all be reviewed case by case.

What happens after I send the form?

We contact you, gather the missing boat details, review title or documentation questions, and explain the practical next step before anything moves forward.
