Donate a Boat to Charity

Boats for Charity allows you to donate your boat without towing, hauling, or storage. Your boat stays exactly where it is. We simply connect donors and buyers so that proceeds support verified charitable programs.

Why Donate a Boat Through Boats for Charity?

  • No towing or transport required
  • No storage or marina coordination
  • You keep control of the boat until ownership is transferred
  • Supports real charitable causes
  • Simple, online-only process

What Types of Boats Can Be Donated?

We accept nearly all types of watercraft, including:

  • Fishing boats
  • Sailboats
  • Cabin cruisers & yachts
  • Jet skis & personal watercraft
  • Non-running or project boats

How the Donation Process Works

  1. You submit your boat information through our website
  2. We list and match your boat to a qualified buyer
  3. Ownership transfers directly between both parties
  4. The charitable portion of the transaction is applied to our programs

Important Note About Transportation

Boats for Charity does not tow, transport, store, or physically move boats. All vessels remain with their current owner until a buyer is matched and ownership is transferred.

Tax Deduction Information

Many donations may qualify for a tax deduction depending on your situation. Always consult your tax professional regarding charitable deductions related to property.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you pick up my boat?

No. Boats for Charity never transports boats. Your vessel remains in place until transferred.

Can I donate a non-running boat?

Yes. Non-running boats are often accepted depending on buyer interest.

How long does the process take?

Timelines vary based on buyer availability, but most listings receive activity within days.
