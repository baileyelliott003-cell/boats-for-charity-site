Thinking About Selling Your Boat? Donating May Be the Better Option

If you’re trying to sell your boat, you already know it can take weeks or months—messages, tire-kickers, storage, and repairs.

For many owners, donating a boat is a faster, simpler alternative that also supports a good cause.

Why selling a boat is harder than most people expect

  • Time spent listing, showing, and negotiating
  • Low offers and no-shows
  • Marina/storage costs while it sits
  • Repair requests after inspections

When donating a boat makes more sense than selling

  • Your boat won’t sell or you’re tired of relisting
  • The boat needs repairs or it’s not worth putting money into
  • You want it gone without dealing with buyers
  • You want your boat to create real impact

Boat donation vs boat selling

SellingDonating
Can take weeks/monthsOften faster and simpler
Messaging + meetups + negotiationsClear steps and paperwork
May require repairs to get offersDonation can make sense when repairs aren’t worth it

How Boats for Charity helps when selling isn’t worth it

Boats for Charity helps donors who are considering selling their boat but want a simpler path. If donating is a fit, we guide you through the donation process and provide donation documentation.

When you should NOT donate your boat

If your boat is in excellent condition and selling it would be quick and profitable, selling may make more sense. We’ll always be honest about whether donation is a good fit.
