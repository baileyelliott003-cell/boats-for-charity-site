Answers Before You Donate

Can I donate a non-running boat?

Yes, many non-running boats can be reviewed. The key details are condition, location, title status, storage situation, and whether the boat has enough value to support the charitable process.

Do I need a clean title?

A clean title or current documentation makes everything easier. If there is a lien, missing paperwork, or an estate issue, tell us in the form so we can review the next step.

What does it cost to ask?

Simply tell us about your boat. We’ll review it, answer your questions, and guide you through the donation process.

What boats do you accept?

We review powerboats, sailboats, cruisers, yachts, personal watercraft, trailers, and documented vessels. Running condition helps, but it is not the only factor.

What happens after I submit?

A real person follows up to confirm details, ask for anything missing, and explain whether the donation looks workable before moving ahead.

Will I receive tax paperwork?

When a donation is completed, we provide donation documentation and tax-related paperwork where applicable. Always confirm deduction questions with your tax professional.
