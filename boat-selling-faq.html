Boat Selling FAQ

Quick answers for people trying to sell a boat — and when donating may be the better option.

Can I donate a boat instead of selling it?

Yes. If selling is taking too long or the boat needs work, donating can be a simpler way to move on while supporting a good cause.

What if my boat won’t sell?

If you’re getting low offers, no‑shows, or the boat has been listed for weeks, donating is often a practical alternative.

Do you take boats that need repairs?

Often, yes. Tell us the condition and we’ll let you know if it’s a fit.

Do I need a trailer?

Not always. Let us know what you have and where the boat is located.

Will I get a receipt?

Yes. We provide donation documentation/receipt for your records. (Talk to a tax professional about your situation.)
