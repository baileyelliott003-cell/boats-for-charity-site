How to Donate a Boat to Charity

Step 1 — Submit Your Boat Information

Tell us the boat type, location, condition, and ownership details.

Step 2 — We Match a Buyer

Your boat is connected with buyers who support charitable funding.

Step 3 — Direct Ownership Transfer

The boat never moves. Ownership transfers directly between parties.

Step 4 — Donation Records Provided

You receive documentation for your records depending on the transaction structure.

Why Boats for Charity Uses a No-Transport Model

By avoiding towing and storage, we reduce costs, simplify logistics, and allow more funds to reach charitable programs.
