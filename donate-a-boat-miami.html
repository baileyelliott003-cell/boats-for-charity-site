Boats for Charity helps donors in Miami donate boats without towing, hauling, or transport. Your boat stays with you while we connect qualified buyers and facilitate a charitable transaction.

Why Donate a Boat in Miami Through Boats for Charity?

  • No towing required
  • No marina or yard storage fees
  • Direct owner-to-buyer transfer
  • Supports verified charitable programs
  • Fast, fully remote process

Boats We Commonly Accept in Miami

  • Sailboats
  • Cruisers & yachts
  • Jet skis
  • Project & non-running boats
